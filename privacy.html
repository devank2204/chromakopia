Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Last Updated: August 2025

Introduction

At Chromakopia Font Generator, protecting your privacy is fundamental to our mission. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data collection, usage, and protection when you use our service.

1. Information Collection

Chromakopia Font Generator operates under a strict no-storage policy. We are committed to not collecting or storing your personal information. Specifically, we do not:

  • Collect personal information or identifiable data
  • Require account registration or sign-ups
  • Store or retain generated images
  • Keep uploaded background images on our servers
  • Track individual user activity or behavior

2. Analytics

We utilize Google Analytics to gather anonymous usage insights that help us improve our service. This data may include:

  • Page views and session duration on our website
  • Device type and browser information
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Traffic sources and referral information

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking through your browser settings or by using privacy-focused browser extensions.

3. Advertising

Our service may display third-party advertisements. These advertising partners may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to deliver personalized ads based on general user interests and browsing patterns.

We do not collect, store, or share personal information with advertisers or advertising networks.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure proper website functionality. Additionally:

  • Google Analytics and advertising partners may set their own cookies for analytics and advertising purposes
  • You have full control to manage or block cookies through your browser settings

5. Third-Party Services

Chromakopia Font Generator integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (for anonymous usage statistics and website optimization)
  • Advertising networks (for displaying relevant advertisements)

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy, which we recommend reviewing for complete transparency.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal information, all image generation and customization processes occur directly within your web browser. Your content remains completely private and is never transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere outside your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not specifically designed for or directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect, process, or store information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. When changes are made, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the "Last Updated" date accordingly.

Contact: If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.
